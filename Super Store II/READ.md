# Super Store II: Regional Profitability Storytelling Analysis

This Tableau project uses interactive visualizations and data storytelling techniques to explore regional and category-level profitability within a retail superstore dataset. Through a sequence of dashboards, filters, annotations, and calculated fields, the analysis guides stakeholders from high-level business trends to specific opportunities for improving performance across geographic markets and product categories.

## Project Overview

The objective of this project was to analyze sales and profitability performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments within a retail superstore environment.

Using Tableau, a series of interconnected visualizations were developed to identify trends, uncover profit drivers, and communicate actionable business insights. The project emphasizes storytelling through data, enabling stakeholders to move from broad performance trends to specific operational and strategic opportunities.

## Business Question

Which regions, product categories, and business segments contribute most significantly to profitability, and what opportunities exist to improve performance across underperforming areas?
